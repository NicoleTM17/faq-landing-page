Do you have a physical store I can visit, or is it online-only?

Currently, our products are only sold online. We do hope to make our products available in stores in the future!

Are your products vegan and cruelty-free?

All of our products are vegan and cruelty free.

Are your fragrances made with natural or synthetic ingredients?

All of our fragrances are made using natural ingredients.

Are your products tested on animals?

We are proud to state that our products are not tested on animals. We are dedicated to cruelty-free practices and do not conduct any animal testing at any stage of our product development. We believe in the importance of ethical and humane treatment of animals, and our commitment to this principle extends to our entire range of products. Rest assured that you can enjoy our fragrances with the peace of mind that no animals were harmed in their creation.

How can I updated my email address?

To edit or change the email address associated with your account, navigate to your profile, select “Personal info,” then select Save after making the appropriate changes.
Having trouble updating your email address? Please contact our Customer Experience team for assistance.

I need to update my profile information, how do I do that?

To make changes to your account information on our website, simply log into your account, then click your initials in the upper-right hand corner (or if you’re using our mobile app, click the Account icon in the lower right-hand corner), and a menu will appear. From there, you can select any of the links to manage your subscription, update your billing details, change your shipping information, or change your name or preferred gender by clicking the corresponding links.

How do I delete my account?

To delete your account, simply log into your account then go to settings. At the bottom of the page there will be an option to delete your account.

Where can I check my subscription details?

To check out your subscription information, simply log into your account and navigate to the settings menu where there will be an option titled 'Your Subscription plan'. There you will find all the information you need.
If you need more help, Please contact our Customer Experience team for assistance.

How do I cancel my subscription?

To cancel your membership, please contact our Customer Experience team here. Once your subscription has been canceled, you will receive an email confirming your subscription's cancellation.

Where can I check my order history?

You can check details of your past orders in your account settings, under the your orders menu.

Where can I find my billing information?

You can find your billing information in your account settings, under the your personal details option.

I think I have been charged incorrectly. How can I contact you?

If you believe there has been an error with your billing, please contact our Customer Experiemce team here.

Where do you ship?

We ship worldwide!

Help, I never received my order!

If you have been waiting a long time for your order with no updates, please contact our customer Experience team here.

My order is faulty, what do I do?

If you have received a faulty item, please contact our customer experience team here.

I want to return my order. Can I get a full refund?

Sure! We're 100% committed to making you happy. If you wish to return your item(s), please contact us with the reason of return, within 10 days from receiving your order (according to the delivery date on the tracking record, if applicable), and we will provide the return instructions in details! Returns without prior communication with us will not be accepted. Returns made will be refunded in your original form of payment.

I want to exchange my order.

Sure! Please contact us with the reason of return, within 10 days from receiving your order (according to the delivery date on the tracking record, if applicable), and we will provide the return instructions in details! Please note that we only accept one-time exchange.

Do you provide a return label?

We do provide return labels if you contact us within 10 days from receiving your order.
